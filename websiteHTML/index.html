Home page

 

The player must decide to complete 5 errands from the list of 9 errands below to prepare for a night out. He must make a choice at each errand i.e. what type of clothes to buy at the clothing store. Each choice he makes will be assigned a numeric value. These "points" need to be calculated and tallied up at the end, and displayed so the player can see his final score.

As mentioned, the player must complete 5 errands. If the player has not completed 5 errands, then he returns to the home page via a customized acknowledgement page until 5 errands are complete. Once he completes 5 errands, the player will be directed to the "end.html" page, where his final score will be displayed.
